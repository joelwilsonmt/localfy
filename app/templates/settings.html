Download Configuration

Set via the DOWNLOAD_PATH environment variable.
YouTube audio is ~128 kbps AAC or 160 kbps Opus — that's the quality ceiling regardless of format. opus and m4a are the best choices: ffmpeg can remux the original stream without re-encoding, avoiding the generation loss of transcoding to mp3. mp3 has the widest device compatibility. Lossless formats (flac, wav) produce much larger files with no quality benefit.

Sync Behaviour

When enabled, songs removed from a Spotify playlist will also be deleted from your local download folder on the next sync. When disabled (default), local files are kept even if you remove the track from Spotify.

Spotify Account

Your Spotify account is connected. Disconnecting will remove the stored token and you'll need to log in again.

About

localfy uses spotdl to download tracks from YouTube and tag them with Spotify metadata.
Downloads are scheduled via APScheduler and run inside this container.
Sync state is stored in /data/localfy.db.
